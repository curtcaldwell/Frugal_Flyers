Colombia

With a culture as vibrant as its diverse landscape, Colombia offers something for everyone. Coastlines on both the pacific and carribean, the Amazon rainforest, and strethes of the Andes Mountains make Colombia the second most biodiverse country in the world. Just like the term 'Magical Realism', famously coined by Colombian nobel laureate Gabriel Garcia Marquez, Colombia is a place where you can find magic and wonder as you connect with the people and nature on your way through this breathtaking land.

Getting Here

With the recent influx of tourism to Colombia, the government has been remodeling and expanding its airports. Daily direct flights from Europe, North and South America to Bogotá or Medellín make it easy to arrive.

Transportation

Once here transportation is fairly easy. Smaller budget airlines like VivaColombia make it very cheap to get around, a roundtrip flight from Bogotá to Cartagena is usually around $100USD if bought in advance. While the government has put a lot funds in the Countries highway system, the mountainous landscape makes a seemingly short trip take a lot longer. However there are plenty of great bus options that shouldnt be passed up if you have the time.
